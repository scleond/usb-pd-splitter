# Fabrication notes

- Two-layer 1.6 mm FR-4, 1 oz copper.
- Green solder mask and white silkscreen.
- Lead-free ENIG surface finish.
- Fabricate from the supplied Gerber X2/Excellon package.
- Board outline is defined only by `Edge.Cuts`.
- Do not scale, reinterpret, or regenerate manufacturing data.
- Resolve discrepancies against the release manifest before fabrication.
