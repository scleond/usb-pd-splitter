# First-article inspection checklist

- [ ] Package revision and checksums match the approved release.
- [ ] Board dimensions, thickness, finish, mask, and silkscreen are correct.
- [ ] No opens or shorts in the fabrication netlist/electrical test.
- [ ] J1-J3 are the specified GCT connector and are fully seated.
- [ ] Connector contacts and shell tabs have complete solder joints.
- [ ] R1-R2 values and placements match the BOM.
- [ ] No unintended population of DNP references.
- [ ] VBUS is isolated from DATA_IN as designed.
- [ ] USB D+/D- continuity and absence of shorts are verified.
- [ ] Visual inspection photographs are archived with the release record.
