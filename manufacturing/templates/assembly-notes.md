# Assembly notes

- Populate every non-DNP reference in the released BOM and CPL.
- Do not populate references marked DNP in KiCad or the assembly drawings.
- USB-C connectors J1-J3 are GCT USB4110-GF-A (JLCPCB C5143397).
- D1 is a green NationStar NCD0805C1 LED (JLCPCB C84257).
- R1-R2 are 5.1 kOhm, 1%, 0805 UNI-ROYAL 0805W8F5101T5E (C27834).
- R3 is a 10 kOhm, 1%, 0805 UNI-ROYAL 0805W8F1002T5E (C17414).
- Verify connector shell seating, coplanarity, and all fine-pitch solder joints.
- Clean flux residue and inspect for bridges before electrical test.
